WannaCry Ransomware Attack
What Happened?

WannaCry was a ransomware attack that spread worldwide in 2017. It encrypted users' files and demanded payment in Bitcoin to restore access.

Which Part of CIA Triad Was Affected?

Availability was mainly affected because users could not access their files.

Integrity was also affected because files were modified through encryption.

Impact
More than 200,000 computers infected.
Hospitals, businesses, and government organizations were affected.
Financial losses occurred worldwide.

Prevention
Regular software updates.
Strong endpoint security.
Regular backups.
Employee security awareness training.
